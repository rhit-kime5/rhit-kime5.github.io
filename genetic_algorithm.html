Type:         Final Project

Course:      Object-Oriented Software Development

Objective:  A Generic Evolutionary Simulator for
                  Optimization and Computational Biology

Duration:   Jan 2021 - Feb 2021

Source Code: Available

Designed and implemented a genetic algorithm and its evolutionary simulator, with customizable generation count, population size, genome length, mutation rate, termination, fitness type, selection method, elitism, and crossover;

Displayed the progress of the evolution as a dynamic graph of the highest, lowest, and average fitness in each generation as well as its diversity, with user-specified evolution speed;

Enhanced GUI with options to visualize the whole population or the best individual, able to edit the genome by user clicks or mutation then saved or loaded as desired.
