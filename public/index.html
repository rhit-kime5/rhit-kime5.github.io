About

I intend to make the world a safer place by strengthening our defense against Weapons of Mass Destruction and reinforcing deterrence via technologically superior, non-NBC asymmetric warfare. In the entirety of human history that has accompanied the never-ending race between the “spear” and the “shield”, this is truly an unprecedented era in which our offensive capabilities are incomparably greater than our defensive capabilities. Whereas the spear-shield race has always returned to its equilibrium in a matter of decades, the current imbalance on the spear with Nuclear, Biological, and Chemical (NBC) weapons has not faced a significant change in the past century. Therefore, supporting the shield in this era to catch up with the spear is taken as a problem of utmost importance to me, not to mention the very real possibility of our self-annihilation.
